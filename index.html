FAQ

Common questions

What is ScoutIQ?

A predictive scouting app for Colorado elk hunters. Instead of static maps, it forecasts daily elk movement by combining terrain, habitat, weather, hunting pressure, and lunar data.

Is ScoutIQ free?

Yes. It is free during the Colorado public beta. Create an account to get access.

How is it different from onX or GOHUNT?

Mapping apps show you the land and access. ScoutIQ adds a prediction layer on top, forecasting where elk are likely to be and when they are likely to move based on current conditions.

What states and species does it cover?

The beta covers elk across Colorado units on public land. More states and species are planned as the model improves.
