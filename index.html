FAQ

Common questions

What is ScoutIQ?

A predictive scouting app for Colorado elk hunters. Instead of static maps and old pins, it hands you a daily movement forecast for your unit, with a confidence level, so you know where to start.

Is ScoutIQ really free?

Yes. Free during the Colorado public beta, every unit, no credit card. Some services charge hundreds of dollars for a single unit's hotspots. We are not doing that.

How is it different from onX or GOHUNT?

You still get the map, layers, and land ownership you expect. What is new is the forecast on top: where elk are likely to be and when they are likely to move, based on current conditions.

What states and species does it cover?

The beta covers elk across Colorado units on public land. More states and species are planned as the model improves.
