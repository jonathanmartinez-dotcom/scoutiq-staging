How it works

Six engines, one forecast.

ScoutIQ is not a map with layers. It is an intelligence engine that reads the mountain the way an experienced hunter does, at the scale of an entire unit.

T

Terrain

Finds saddles, benches, drainages, spurs, and glassing knobs that funnel elk movement.

H

Habitat

Reads forest type, burn scars, water, and feed to score where elk want to be.

W

Weather

Pulls real-time conditions and recent fronts that push elk on their feet.

P

Pressure

Weighs hunting and recreation pressure that moves elk off predictable patterns.

M

Movement

Predicts daily activity windows: bedding, feeding, travel, and peak times.

L

Learning

Improves with every field report, verified sign, and trail-cam observation.

Confidence, not guarantees

Every score says how sure we are

Wildlife movement is probabilistic. ScoutIQ shows a movement forecast with a confidence level, never a promise. That keeps it honest and gets sharper as more field data comes in.
